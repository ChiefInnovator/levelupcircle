Level-Up Circle Generator

The Level-Up Circle Generator is a free, interactive web tool for creating radial synergy diagrams. A Level-Up Circle visualizes how your goals, projects, and focus areas interconnect and reinforce each other — turning isolated objectives into a compounding flywheel of growth.

What is a Level-Up Circle?

A Level-Up Circle is a circular diagram where each node represents a goal or focus area. Lines connecting the nodes show how progress in one area drives progress in others. This approach is inspired by flywheel thinking used by companies like Amazon and Disney, where interconnected efforts create self-reinforcing momentum. Unlike a simple goal list, a Level-Up Circle reveals the synergy between your objectives.

Key Features

  • Up to 36 labeled focus areas arranged in a radial pattern
  • Smart color generation using golden-angle and equal spacing algorithms
  • Dark and light themes with customizable neon glow effects
  • High-resolution export up to 8192×8192 pixels (8K) at 300 DPI
  • Export in PNG, WebP, and AVIF formats with embedded metadata
  • Instant sharing via deep links, Twitter/X, and Facebook
  • Ask ChatGPT for strategic advice based on your focus areas
  • Fullscreen presentation mode
  • Auto-save with localStorage persistence
  • No sign-up, download, or installation required

How to Use

Open the tool in any modern browser. Enter a title, add focus areas (up to 36), customize colors and glow effects, then export as a high-resolution image or share via URL. The tool saves your work automatically. Use the Ask ChatGPT feature to get personalized strategic advice on creating synergy between your focus areas.

Who Is It For?

The Level-Up Circle Generator is for anyone practicing strategic goal setting, personal development, entrepreneurship, or project planning. It helps coaches, founders, students, and professionals visualize the interconnections between their goals and design a system where each win reinforces the next.
